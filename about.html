Limitation of the Simulator

This simulator is designed for educational purposes and provides a simplified representation of network components and their interactions. It does not encompass all the complexities of real-world networking, such as advanced routing protocols, security features, or performance optimizations. Users should be aware that while the simulator offers a foundational understanding of network concepts, it may not accurately reflect the behavior of actual network devices and configurations.

Each Subnet needs its own router as a gateway to connect to other subnets.
